The company

Built by operators,
for operators.

NLMA was founded because the right software rarely existed off the shelf. So we started building it — first for ourselves, then for everyone else.

Next Level Management Advisors started as a management and operations consultancy helping small businesses untangle the systems holding them back. Over time the work kept converging on the same truth: the tooling gap was the problem. Off-the-shelf platforms handled 80% of the job and left operators duct-taping the rest.

We pivoted into building the missing 20% — custom integrations, AI-powered workflows, and infrastructure that runs itself. Today NLMA ships production software and agent automation across real estate, property management, government, and commercial sectors.

We are a small, founder-led firm based in Virginia. We move fast, own the outcomes, and do not outsource judgment.
