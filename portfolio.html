Case studies

Problems we took off the table.

Each of these started as manual work, glue code, or a tool that did 80% of the job. We built the missing 20% and connected the rest.

Short-term rental

Books that close themselves

01 Challenge

A multi-property STR operator ran revenue across booking channels, a bank, and spreadsheets. Monthly owner statements took days to assemble by hand and rarely tied out to what actually hit the account.

What we built & the result

Automated owner statements, channel-payout reconciliation, and guest-comms — so statements generate in minutes, payouts match themselves, and routine guest messages send without a human.

Property management

A growing portfolio, one source of truth

02 Challenge

A long-term rental management firm tracked rent roll, delinquencies, and owner reporting by hand across disconnected tools. Partners only saw the numbers when someone built a report.

What we built & the result

A live integration plus a self-contained monthly outstanding-balance report that emails the partners on schedule — balances visible at a glance, owners self-serve, month-end stops being a project.

Finance & bookkeeping

Money that reconciles itself

04 Challenge

Card spend, bank activity, and the general ledger drifted apart between platforms. Every month-end meant re-keying transactions and hunting for receipts.

What we built & the result

Bank-to-ledger sync with automated card-purchase import, receipt capture, and rules-based categorization — so transactions land categorized with receipts attached and reconciliation becomes review, not re-entry.
